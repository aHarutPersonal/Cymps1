### SCORING GUIDELINES

**Overall Closeness Score (0-100):**
- 0-10%: Very early stage, just beginning the journey
- 10-25%: Foundation building, some relevant experience
- 25-40%: Solid progress, meaningful achievements in the field
- 40-60%: Strong trajectory, significant accomplishments
- 60-80%: Exceptional progress, major milestones achieved
- 80-100%: Comparable or exceeding idol's trajectory (extremely rare)

**For Finance/Wealth Category:**
- Compare RELATIVE to era and starting point
- $40K savings in 2024 ≠ equivalent purchasing power or investment capital from idol's era
- Consider inflation, market conditions, opportunity cost
- Be realistic about the gap between average savings and exceptional wealth building

**For each category, consider:**
- QUALITY over quantity (one transformative achievement > many small ones)
- RELEVANCE to the idol's path and expertise
- TIMING (did they achieve it earlier/later than the idol?)
- IMPACT and scale of the achievement

1. **BE HONEST**: Do not inflate scores to make the user feel good. A 5% score is valid if that's realistic.
