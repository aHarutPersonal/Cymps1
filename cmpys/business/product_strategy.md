# CMPYS Product Strategy

**Owner:** Product Strategy Agent  
**Status:** Source of truth for the current roadmap  
**Last updated:** 2026-05-13

## Product Spine

CMPYS is built around one coherent loop:

1. **Agentic activation:** the user gives minimum context, picks a mentor, completes a short diagnostic interview, and sees an age-matched comparison.
2. **12-week execution:** the strategic blueprint becomes a concrete plan with weekly missions, daily rhythm tasks, lessons, resources, and completion criteria.
3. **Daily retention:** Today gives the user one focused action, reading continuation, reflection, and mentor support.

The blueprint is a strategic verdict, not a second roadmap. The 12-week plan is the execution artifact.

## Canonical First-Run Journey

1. Auth or profile setup.
2. Agentic intake: age, current life context, interests.
3. Three AI mentor suggestions.
4. Mentor selection.
5. Live interview.
6. Mirror comparison and strategic blueprint.
7. Generate or open the 12-week plan.
8. Land on Today.

Longer achievement intake and profile enrichment happen after the first value moment.

## Daily Use Journey

1. Open Today.
2. Review daily focus and streak.
3. Complete the plan task or continue reading.
4. Save a reflection.
5. Ask the Mentor for help when blocked.
6. Review the weekly summary.

## Positioning

**Promise:** See where you stand. Learn what they did. Close the gap.

**Category:** AI mentor and learning planner for ambitious people who model themselves after real role models.

**Differentiator:** age-matched comparison plus domain-specific daily practice grounded in the mentor's actual methods.

## Personas

**Aspiring Achiever, 25-35:** knows the domain or idol they care about, values depth, will spend 20-60 minutes per day.

**Curious Explorer, 18-24:** wants fast motivation and guidance, needs quick wins, streaks, and accessible mentor choices.

## Monetization Assumptions

These are planning assumptions, not implemented entitlements:

- Free: basic onboarding, mentor suggestions, first comparison preview.
- Paid: full strategic blueprint, 12-week execution plan, deep lessons, daily coaching, extended mentor chat.
- Usage limits should protect LLM cost for grounded search, plan generation, detailed lessons, and chat.
- Upgrade triggers should appear after the emotional comparison and before deep execution value.

## Success Metrics

- Idol suggestion completion.
- Mentor selection rate.
- Interview completion.
- Comparison viewed.
- Plan generated.
- Daily focus viewed and completed.
- Reflection saved.
- Day-1 and Day-7 retention.
- First week completion.
- Content accepted below quality threshold.
- LLM cost per activated user.
